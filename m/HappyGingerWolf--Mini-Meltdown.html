Mini-Meltdown

A mod for those who hate the balance of the lategame. This mod adds a single turret, the mini meltdown. The mini meltdown behaves exactly like a regular meltdown, except for one small change; it fits in a 1x1 tile. Same damage, range, cost, and power consumption, but in a smaller package. I advise turning off sound when using this mod.
