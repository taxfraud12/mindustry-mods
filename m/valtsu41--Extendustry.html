Extendustry

A mindustry mod that anyone can add content to

About

The idea is this: as long as it is nothing stupid or unbalanced (like a something that sings political songs or shoots an infinite laser in survival) you can add it to this mod. Contribution guide can be found in file CONTRIBUTING.md.

Main branches

  • master The stable branch that is the recommended version to use.
  • development The unstable branch where the developing happens.
